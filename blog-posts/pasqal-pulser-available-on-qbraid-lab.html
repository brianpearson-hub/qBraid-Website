Pulser, Pasqal’s quantum SDK, is available on qBraid Lab

20254 min read

Pulser is ready to use on qBraid Lab with zero setup, so you can program neutral atom devices faster.

Highlights

  • Prebuilt Pulser environments—no installs required
  • Sample notebooks and tutorials to get started quickly
  • Run side-by-side with other SDKs for benchmarking

Launch Pulser in minutes and focus on designing experiments instead of configuring environments.
