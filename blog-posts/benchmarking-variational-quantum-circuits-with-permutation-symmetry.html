Benchmarking variational quantum circuits with permutation symmetry

20254 min read

Symmetry-aware benchmarking

We benchmarked variational circuits that enforce permutation symmetry to study fidelity and convergence across QPUs and simulators on qBraid Lab.

Key findings

  • Symmetric ansätze reduce parameter counts while preserving expressivity
  • Hardware noise interacts differently with symmetric structures, so qBraid Runtime scheduling matters
  • Benchmark data aides in selecting backends for each problem class

These benchmarks help practitioners choose the right hardware and optimization strategy for their variational tasks.
