qBraid Announces qBraid SDK Integrated with Amazon Braket on qBraid Lab

December 2025 6 min read

Agnostic access to quantum devices

qBraid SDK now integrates with Amazon Braket, providing unified access to leading quantum computers and simulators from a single, Python-native interface.

What’s included

  • Cross-framework transpilation and device-agnostic APIs
  • Access to neutral-atom, superconducting, and photonic backends via Braket
  • Real-time status monitoring, job orchestration, and priority queues

Why it matters

Developers can prototype faster, switch hardware with minimal code changes, and run hybrid workflows on the same platform used by 25K+ qBraid Lab users.
