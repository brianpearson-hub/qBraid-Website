qBraid’s Wins 1st for the Best Quantum Algorithm Paper Award at IEEE

20255 min read

Recognizing quantum algorithm excellence

IEEE QCE 2023 honored qBraid and academic collaborators with first place for advances in variational quantum algorithms built on qBraid Runtime and SDK.

Why it matters

  • Hybrid techniques combining classical solvers with qBraid Runtime orchestration
  • Device-agnostic execution that spans QPUs and GPU-accelerated simulations
  • Community collaboration that pushes open-source algorithm research forward

The award reflects qBraid’s leadership in making research-ready quantum algorithms accessible to developers worldwide.
