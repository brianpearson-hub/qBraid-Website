qBraid partners with Quantum Algorithms Institute

20255 min read

Growing the Pacific Northwest quantum community

qBraid and Quantum Algorithms Institute partner to accelerate the pace of quantum computing in British Columbia and beyond.

What we’re doing together

  • Providing pilots and labs that showcase qBraid Lab plus QuEra and other partners
  • Training students with ready-to-run notebooks and guided problem sets
  • Sharing analytics and co-mentoring to accelerate quantum innovation

The partnership bridges government, academia, and industry to accelerate quantum talent pipelines.
